Current Projects

Agent Arena

An interactive platform for evaluating and comparing LLM-based agents. The project introduces a novel battle-based comparison framework and ranking algorithm for systematic assessment of agent capabilities.

Collaborators: Shishir Patil, Joseph Gonzalez

Project Page

Ember: Compositional AI Systems

A framework for constructing Compound AI Systems and Networks of Networks (NONs), enabling scalable and modular AI workflows. The project focuses on improving the reliability and composability of AI systems.

Collaborators: Jared Davis, Matei Zaharia

GitHub

Emotion-tune

Research on improving RLHF with emotion-based feedback, built on EMILI (Emotionally Intelligent Listener). The project uses real-time facial emotion recognition to enhance AI interactions and advance long-term AI safety.
