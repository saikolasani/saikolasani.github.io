About Me

Hey, I'm Sai. I'm currently a 4th-year undergrad at UC Berkeley, pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and Statistics with a minor in Public Policy. I am broadly interested in artificial intelligence and machine learning systems. More specifically, my interests include:

  • Developing techniques for AI alignment
  • Improving AI reliability and safety
  • Building open source frameworks

Currently, I'm:

  • Co-developing Agent Arena at the Sky Computing Lab under Shishir Patil and Joseph Gonzalez
  • Leading Ember development with Jared Davis and Matei Zaharia
  • Working as an Investment Research Analyst at Kindred Ventures, evaluating early-stage startups in AI, SaaS, and digital health

Previously, I interned as a software engineer at Arize AI, DataStax, and Priceline.

Featured Research Projects

  • Agent Arena: An interactive platform for evaluating and comparing LLM-based agents. Agent Arena provides a systematic way to assess agent capabilities through battle-based comparisons
  • Ember: A compositional framework for constructing Compound AI Systems and Networks of Networks (NONs), focusing on scalable and modular AI workflows
  • Emotion-tune: A project focused on improving RLHF with emotion-based feedback. Built on EMILI (Emotionally Intelligent Listener), it uses real-time facial emotion recognition to enhance AI interactions and advance long-term AI safety through emotion-aware systems
